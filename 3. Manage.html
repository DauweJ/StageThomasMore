M.3: Discontinue processing on request (Art: 7(3); 21(1-4); 30(4))

When requested by a data subject, can the organization discontinue processing some forms of personal data?

1. Discontinue processing all forms of a data subject's personal data (particularly direct marketing) when requested by the data subject and deemed appropriate by the organization?

2. Provide data subjects with notice and justification for the continued use of their personal data, when a request to discontinue use is rejected?

3. Record and maintain evidence of discontinued personal data use?

4. Use an established process to consistently and promptly respond to requests from data subjects to stop using their data?

5. Automatically perform all of the above activities?
