M.10: Review data processing conducted by automated means.

Can the organization identify decisions (e.g. credit checks, background checks) for data subjects that are performed completely or partially by automated means?

For decisions made via automated processing:

1. Are the automated decisions evaluated by legal and compliance personnel to establish proper business justification and rationale?

2. Is there a policy in place to identify when human intervention is necessary to review automated decisions?

3. Is there a defined procedure for human intervention for automated decisions that are prone to inconsistency?

4. Is there a defined process to allow data subjects to explain, challenge, or express a point of view on a decision?
