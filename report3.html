R.3: Track and record flows of personal data to third-party service providers.

Does the organization maintain an inventory of processes that transmit personal data to third-party service providers?

Does the organization:

1. Assess potential third-party service providers for adherence to personal data requirements?

2. Document which third-party service provider’s process personal data, and define personal data protection requirements for all applicable third-parties?

3. Embed personal data protection requirements within contracts and agreements with third-party service providers?

4. Establish procedures for auditing third-party providers' compliance with agreements and controls?

5. Maintain ongoing communication with third-party service providers about personal data processing requirements?
