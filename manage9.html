M.9: Restrict the processing of personal dat

Have a procedure and policy been established to restrict processing of personal data, when required?

For that personal data, does the organization:

1. Have the ability to suspend or restrict processing activities on request?

2. Have procedures to notify additional recipients or processors to restrict processing?

3. Automatically notify recipients of processing activity restrictions?

4. Have a process and technology to notify data subjects if a restriction of processing has been lifted?

5. Automatically notify applicable data subjects when processing activities have been resumed after restriction?

6. Maintain a record of instances when processing activities were restricted?

7. Maintain a record of instances where processing activities were restricted and then resumed, including the explanation?
