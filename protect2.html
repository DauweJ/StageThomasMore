Protect - P2: Secure personal data through encryption.

The following questions are about data protection and privacy.

Is the organization aware of technologies to encrypt personal data and has it encrypted some personal data, such as government identification numbers, birth dates, or banking numbers?

Has the organization:

1. Have a policy or procedure in place to define what personal data to encrypt, how to encrypt it, and the purpose of encryption?

2. Maintain a data protection standard that documents encryption criteria?

3. Have appropriate technologies in place to perform encryption?

4. Regularly analyze new encryption technology and keep up to date with strong encryption?
