R.2: Track and record flows of personal data into and out of the EU

Does the organization have documentation of ongoing personal data transfers into and out of the EU?

Does the organization:

1. Maintain a record of all processing activities that involve personal data transfer into and out of the EU, including records of ad-hoc transfers that are not part of an ongoing process?

2. Have a process to stay up to date with changing requirements for international transfers, including which countries or organizations ensure an adequate level of data protection as decided by the EU?

3. Have appropriate personnel in place to support tracking and recording personal data transfers across international boundaries?

4. Have technology in place to track and record geographical transfers of personal data, including documenting to which country the data was transferred and what safeguards were used?

5. Have defined processes in place to track and record geographical transfers of personal data?
