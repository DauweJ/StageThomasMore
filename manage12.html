M.12: Define enterprise risk management strategy, inclusive of data privacy risks.

Does the organization maintain a risk management program that includes considerations for data privacy?

Does the risk management program:

1. Maintain principles and guidelines for addressing risk across the organization?

2. Include a defined framework to assess and manage threats across the organization?

3. Define mitigation or transfer strategies, as necessary?

4. Prioritize risk to focus resources on protecting and securing the highest value business assets?

5. Include considerations (whether financial, reputational, or otherwise) for risks of mishandling personal data?
