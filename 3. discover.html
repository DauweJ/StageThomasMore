D.3: Maintain an inventory of personal data holdings

Does the organization have a tool to catalog how and where personal data is used, and is it partially or fully populated?

Does the organization have:

1. A complete inventory of how and where personal data is used with all instances documented?

2. Technology in place to automate or partially automate updates to the inventory?

3. A process that is used regularly to keep the inventory up to date?

4. An inventory of all processing activities where personal data is being obtained?

5. Documented details of each processing activity including scope, purpose, and criteria for when notifications and consent are required?
