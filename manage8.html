M.8: Provide data subject with their personal data in a common, structured format.

Is a mechanism established to provide data subjects a copy of their personal data, including in an electronic format?

Is this data provided:

1. In a common, machine readable format, such as an .xls or .xml file?

2. Automatically to the data subject in an appropriate format?

3. In a format that can be sent to another controller, when requested by the data subject?
