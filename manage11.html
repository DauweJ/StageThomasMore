M.11: Appoint a DPO

Is there a person appointed as the Data Protection Officer (DPO)?

Does the DPO:

1. Conduct privacy training at regular, defined intervals for all relevant personnel?

2. Maintain regular communications with internal counterparts and external peers in his or her professional network responsible for data privacy?

3. Perform independent review and oversight of data privacy activities?

4. Stay up to date with regulatory requirements and maintain privacy expertise?

5. Provide guidance on defining and maintaining roles and responsibilities of data privacy positions within the organization?

6. Review all necessary compliance regulations for data privacy requirements, based on GDPR and other relevant regulations?
