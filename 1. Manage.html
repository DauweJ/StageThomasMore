Manage

The following questions are about data protection and privacy.

M.1: Enable data governance practices and processes

Does the organization have a data governance program?

Does the data governance program include:

1. An organizational structure and formal charter for carrying out the program in a consistent manner?

2. Integration across departments to ensure data governance is consistent and effective organization-wide?

3. Data privacy and protection policies?

4. Technology to protect against, monitor, and report on privacy and protection policy violations?

5. Specific protections for children's personal data?

6. Policies that enforce accountability within the organization?

7. Legal justification documented for using special categories of personal data (racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs, or trade union membership, and the processing of genetic data, biometric data for the purpose of uniquely identifying a natural person, data concerning health or data concerning a natural person's sex life or sexual orientation)?
