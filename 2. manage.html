M.2: Provide detailed notice processing activities to data subjects (Art: 7(2); 12(1); 13(1-3); 14(1-4))

Does the organization provide data subjects with privacy notices that describe how their data is used?

Are the privacy notices:

1. Written in clear and plain language?

2. Governed by a formal policy and process to ensure they are shared in a timely, consistent, and appropriate manner?

3. Inclusive of required information, such as organization contact details and purposes for using personal data?

4. Shared with data subjects at first point of contact, when informing them they may object to how the organization uses their personal data?

5. Generated and shared by automated means?

6. Shared with data subjects at all points where personal data is collected?

7. Shared with data subjects when personal data is collected from a source other than the data subjects, including online profiles, sites, or other interactions not directly between the data subject and the organization?

8. Shared with data subjects, before the organization uses their personal data for new purposes not already communicated to them?
