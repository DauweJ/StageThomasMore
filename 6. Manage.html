M.6 Rectify inaccurate or incomplete personal data regarding data subjects (Art 16; 30(4))

For some cases, can the organization correct inaccuracies or complete partial instances of data subject personal data when requested?

Can the organization:

1. Correct inaccuracies or complete partial instances of all data subject personal data, when requested by the data subject?

2. Record, maintain, and readily share evidence of correcting or completing personal data?

3. Consistently and promptly correct and complete personal data, as well as record and maintain evidence of this action?

4. In some cases, automatically correct and complete personal data, as well as record and maintain evidence of the correction or completion?

5. In all cases, automatically correct and complete personal data, as well as record and maintain evidence of the correction or completion?
