D.2: Facilitate data classification

Can the organization categorize the types of personal data it uses?

Does the organization:

1. Label different categories of data in varying degrees of sensitivity, such as "sensitive," "confidential," or "public"?

2. Label data with the geographic restrictions that may apply?

3. Label the origin of data, i.e. whether data was provided by the data subject or obtained through other means?

4. Perform data classification activities in a consistent and timely manner?

5. Automatically perform all of the above activities?
